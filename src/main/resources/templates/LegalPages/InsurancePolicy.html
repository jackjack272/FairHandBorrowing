FairHandBorrowing Limited Compensation Insurance Policy

Introduction

Thank you for choosing FairHandBorrowing's Limited Compensation Insurance. This policy outlines the terms and conditions under which you can claim limited financial compensation in case of default by the borrower.

Definitions

FairHandBorrowing: Refers to the insurance provider.

Insured: Refers to the individual who has purchased this insurance policy from FairHandBorrowing.

Borrower: Refers to the party who has borrowed funds from the Insured through FairHandBorrowing's lending platform.

Default: Refers to the failure of the Borrower to repay the loan as per the agreed-upon terms and conditions.

Policy Coverage

This insurance policy offers limited financial compensation to the Insured in case of Default by the Borrower. The compensation amount will be subject to the terms mentioned in this policy.

Limitations

The coverage provided by this insurance policy is subject to the following limitations:

  • The Insured must report the Default to FairHandBorrowing within [number of days] from the date of occurrence.
  • The Borrower's Default must be a result of genuine financial hardship or unforeseen circumstances and not due to any fraudulent activities.
  • The maximum compensation amount payable to the Insured will be limited to [amount] or [percentage] of the outstanding loan amount at the time of Default, whichever is lower.
  • Any outstanding fees, interest, or penalties accrued after the Default will not be covered under this policy.

Claim Process

In the event of a Default, the Insured must follow these steps to initiate the claim process:

  1. Notify FairHandBorrowing of the Default within the stipulated time frame.
  2. Submit all necessary documentation related to the loan agreement and the Default incident.
  3. FairHandBorrowing will conduct a thorough investigation to verify the claim's validity.
  4. If the claim is approved, the Insured will receive the limited financial compensation within [number of days] of the claim's approval.

Policy Exclusions

This insurance policy does not cover the following situations:

  • Defaults that occurred before the effective date of this policy.
  • Defaults that are a result of the Insured's failure to adhere to FairHandBorrowing's lending policies and procedures.
  • Defaults caused by acts of war, terrorism, or natural disasters.

Policy Termination

The insurance policy will be terminated under the following circumstances:

  • If the Insured requests cancellation in writing.
  • If FairHandBorrowing discovers any misrepresentation, fraudulent activity, or non-disclosure of relevant information by the Insured.
  • If the Insured no longer has an active lending account with FairHandBorrowing.
